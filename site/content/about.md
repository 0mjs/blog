Hi, I'm Matt 👋🏼.<br>
I'm a software engineer focused on backend services, distributed architectures, and building secure, reliable software that real people use day-to-day.

I work primarily with TypeScript professionally, and have done for around eight years now. I dabble in Go and Python, with a particular soft-spot for Go, as somewhat of a second language that I've picked up recently.

In my spare time and on the weekends, I build fun products like [Nuddge](https://nuddge.app), and recently, [Flancer](https://flancer.dev) - along with OSS projects like [Zinc](https://zinc.carbonsoft.sh) ([Zinc - GitHub](https://github.com/0mjs/zinc)). I've also worked on some startups/SaaS throughout the years, in 2021-2022, I worked on a solo AI SaaS called [IconAI](https://www.producthunt.com/products/iconai), which was fairly well received on ProductHunt and gained over 50,000+ unique sign ups in just over a year. In 2023-2025, I helped create an AI MedTech system called Samsa, which was accredited for improving clinical pathways for two large private hospitals in Ireland, where we ran two successful clinical trials. We were included as winners in the [HiHi-AI Call of 2025](https://hih.ie/initiatives/hihi-ai/hihi-ai-call-winners-2025/samsa-ltd/). In 2026, I've started my next "side hustle" which is called [Carbon Software](https://carbonsoftware.vercel.app), where I plan to house my latest works under one unified umbrella.
