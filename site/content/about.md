I'm an engineer living in Belfast, Ireland.

I work mostly with TypeScript, and have done for around eight years. I also spend time with Go and Python, with a particular soft spot for Go.

Today I'm building [CutWise](https://cutwise.fit), an AI agent for cutting weight and dieting.

I've built a handful of products and side projects over the years - both SaaS and OSS, including [Nuddge](https://nuddge.app), [QuitShark](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/quitshark-quit-nicotine/id6759577475), [IconAI](https://www.producthunt.com/products/iconai), and [Zinc](https://zinc.carbonsoft.sh).

I also co-founded and lead Samsa Health as CTO, an AI MedTech system that supported clinical pathway improvements across two private hospitals in Ireland and was selected as a winner in the [HiHi-AI Call of 2025](https://hih.ie/initiatives/hihi-ai/hihi-ai-call-winners-2025/samsa-ltd/). I built an OSS library used in the project, for extracting structured data from Healthlink-style PDF documents called [Refrax](https://github.com/0mjs/refrax).

I collect my current work under [Carbon Software](https://carbonsoftware.vercel.app).
