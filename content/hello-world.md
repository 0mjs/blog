## Why Minimalism?

In an era of increasingly complex software systems and frameworks, there's something refreshing about returning to basics. This blog is built with:

- Pure Go and the standard library
- No JavaScript (except where absolutely necessary)
- Markdown for content
- Simple, elegant typography
- Dark mode by default
